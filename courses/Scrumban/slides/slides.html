SrcrumBan

Exploring a hybrid of Scrum and Kanban

Agile

  • Not a methodology!
  • Manifesto with 12 principles
  • Deliver Value!
  • Early and continuously with a flexible framework
🫠

Scrum

  • Iterative framework using sprints (1–2 weeks)
  • Work pulled from prioritized product backlog
  • Regular checkpoints for feedback and adjustment
  • Predictable, structured development cycle
👍

Roles

  • Developers
  • Scrum Master
  • Product Owner
💻

Events

  • Sprint Planning
  • Daily Stand-ups
  • Sprint Review
  • Retrospective
🗓️

Metrics

  • Velocity: Amount of work completed per sprint
  • Story points: Effort values for tasks
  • Focus on consistency across iterations
📈

Kanban

  • Continuous workflow (no fixed iterations)
  • Tasks move through stages
  • Visualized using a task board
  • Identifies bottlenecks in real time
👍

Taskboard

strategies

  • Pull-based system (work starts when capacity allows)
  • Limit Work in Progress (WIP)
  • Focus on steady, efficient workflow
⚖️

Metrics/Structure

  • Cycle Time: Time to complete a task
  • Lead Time: request to delivery time
  • No fixed roles. No fixed events!
  • No scrum!
  • Unless...
📊

Kanban Cadences

  • Customized, regular meetings
    • Daily stand-ups
    • Delivery planning
    • Retrospectives
🗓️

Scrumban

  • Kanban with Scrum-coded cadences
  • Tasks move through defined stages
  • Limits WIP
💡

Roles

  • Business Analyst
  • Team Lead
💻

Events

  • Daily stand-ups
  • Retrospectives
  • Closing meetings
🗓️

Metrics

  • Tasks estimated using Fibonacci sequence
    • 1: Half a day
    • 2: A day
    • 3-5: Two-Three days
    • 8: More than half a week
    • 13: A week
📊
